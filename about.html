About Us

Coexist is a platform that highlights and works towards the issues of animal and ecological welfare. Our mission is to educate and engage communities to build a future in which man and nature live together in harmony. From time to time, this page will focus the spotlight on worthy conservation efforts so we can applaud them and learn from them. However, Coexist does not endorse any individual or organization.

Our Works

CO-EXIST actually means how any flora and fauna is coping with the conditions in which it lives. They were in a good state of welfare if
(as indicated by scientific evidence) it is healthy, comfortable, well-nourished, safe, able to express innate behavior, and if it is not
suffering from unpleasant states such as pain, fear, and distress.

Our Work includes disease prevention and veterinary treatment, appropriate shelter, management, nutrition, humane handling and
humane slaughter. We ensure that the treatment that an animal receives is covered our other terms such as animal care, animal husbandry,
and humane treatment were always be of the best one.

Ensuring flora and fauna protection is every human responsibility that includes consideration for all aspects of animal well-being, including
proper housing, management, nutrition, disease prevention and treatment,responsible care, humane handling, and, when necessary, humane euthanasia.

There are numerous perspectives for taking care of flora and fauna were influenced by a person's values and experiences. There are also various means of
measuring animal welfare, including (but not limited to) health,productivity, behavior, and physiological responses.
